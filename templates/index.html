Our History

In the year 1929, when the world was on the brink of a great depression that was about to unfold, a small town in the South of India saw a visionary who opened the door to a lucky future for his hometown. With a dream of providing quality products and services, he worked hard to open a small retail store selling FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods) to the local community.

As years passed, the business grew and prospered, adapting to the changing times and markets, each generation of the Sahib family carrying forward their great-grandfather's values and legacy with pride, adding their own innovations to the enterprise. Today, Sahibs Trading & Services Company is a well-established and renowned company, with a glorious heritage spanning across generations with diversified operations, operating in the GCC (Gulf Cooperation Council) region, covering Saudi Arabia, the UAE, and Qatar.

As the fourth generation of the Sahib family, we are committed to marching forward till the end of the horizon, upholding the core values of our enterprise: Legacy, Quality, Innovation, and Customer-Centricity.

Our Mission

Our current endeavours involve the passionate pursuit of our vision of establishing a strong foothold in the GCC market, particularly in the flourishing economies of Saudi Arabia, the UAE, and Qatar.

Core Values

At Sahibs, we are driven by the hallmarks of our existence, a touch of which is reflected in everything we do. With the glory of legacy carved along the roots, we aspire to supply our community with nothing less than the best.

Our Quality

From tip to toe, we handle every order with care and accuracy, delivering goods and services of peak quality on time. We source our products from reliable and ethical suppliers, ensuring that they meet the highest standards of safety and quality.
